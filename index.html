I am a doctoral student in the Department of English at the University of Virginia. I work on modern and contemporary literature and culture, with particular focuses on Anglophone modernism, music and musicology, sound studies, the novel, narrative theory, and James Joyce. I have work published or forthcoming in Conradiana, The International Trumpet Guild Journal, and James Joyce Quarterly. I am currently a Praxis Fellow in the Scholars’ Lab. On this website you can find more information about my work and research. For more information, please contact me at bmw9t@virginia.edu. Hello!
